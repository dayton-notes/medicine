# Cerebrovascular Disorders

## Arteriovenous Malformation (AVM)

- Rare, often genetic, usually asymptomatic
    
- Presents with headache, seizures → risk of SAH/stroke
    
- **Treatment:** embolization, surgical resection, medical management
    

## Cerebral Aneurysm

- Commonly at **Circle of Willis** (berry aneurysm) → SAH
    
- Sentinel headache 1–3 weeks prior (~40%)
    
- **Treatment:** coil or clip

# Movement Disorders

## Essential Tremor

- Intention tremor
    
- Worse with stress, improves with alcohol
    
- **Treatment:** none, propranolol, primidone, DBS
    

## Huntington Disease

- Dementia → progressive chorea
    
- Diagnosis: clinical + family history, caudate atrophy on imaging
    
- **Treatment:**
    
    - Phenothiazines (chorea)
        
    - Antipsychotics (behavioral symptoms)
        

## Parkinson Disease

- Resting tremor (“pill rolling”)
    
- Bradykinesia
    
- Rigidity (cogwheeling)
    
- Postural instability (shuffling gait)
    
- **Treatment:** levodopa/carbidopa, COMT inhibitors, dopamine agonists

## Guillain-Barré Syndrome (GBS)

- Symmetric **ascending weakness**
    
- ↓/absent DTRs
    
- **Diagnosis:**
    
    - LP: albuminocytologic dissociation (↑ protein, normal cells/glucose)
        
    - EMG/NCS: slowed conduction velocity
        
- **Treatment:** plasmapheresis or IVIG

- **Diagnosis:** edrophonium (Tensilon) test, ice-pack test
