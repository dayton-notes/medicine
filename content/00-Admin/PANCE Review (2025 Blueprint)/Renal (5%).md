# Neoplasms

- Renal cell carcinoma
    - Rf: TOBACCO, obesity, HTN, exposures
    - Sx: flank pain, hematuria, palpable abdominal mass
    - Dx: CT (enhancing renal lesion is RCC until proven otherwise)
    - Tx: surgery
- Wilms tumor
    - MC pediatric abdominal mass
    - Dx: Abd US / CT, confirmed with path
    - Tx: nephrectomy (if <7cm, partial nephrectomy)
