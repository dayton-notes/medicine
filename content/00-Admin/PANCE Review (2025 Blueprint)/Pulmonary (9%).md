#### Effusions
Effusion is **exudative** if one of the following are met:
- Pleural fluid-to-serum protein ratio > 0.5
- Pleural fluid-to-serum LDH ratio > 0.6
- Pleural fluid LDH > 0.67 (i.e. two-thirds) the upper limits of the laboratory's normal serum LDH

Otherwise: **Transudative**

### Interstitial Lung Disease

**Features**: 
- Poor exercise tolerance / low O2 stores
- end point is irreversible fibrosis
- trajectories differ; protect from pulmonary insults (vaccines!)

**Idiopathic Pulmonary Fibrosis**
- honeycombing, starts in bases
- dry crackles

**Sarcoidosis**
- **multisystem granulomatous disease with a fairly stereotyped clinical pattern.
- Diagnostic: **noncaseating granulomas** on biopsy

Most common features:
- **Bilateral hilar lymphadenopathy** (most classic finding)
- **Pulmonary involvement** (dry cough, dyspnea, interstitial infiltrates)
- **Erythema nodosum** (tender red nodules on shins)
- **Anterior uveitis** (eye pain, photophobia, blurred vision)
- **Hypercalcemia** (↑ vitamin D activation by granulomas)

**Pneumoconiosis**

| Disease                        | Exposure              | Key finding                                    | Big complication               |
| ------------------------------ | --------------------- | ---------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------ |
| **Silicosis**                  | Sand, mining, glass   | **Upper lobe fibrosis + eggshell hilar nodes** | ↑ **TB risk**                  |
| **Coal worker pneumoconiosis** | Coal mining           | **Upper lobe “black lung” nodules**            | Progressive massive fibrosis   |
| **Asbestosis**                 | Shipyards, insulation | **Lower lobe fibrosis + pleural plaques**      | **Mesothelioma / lung cancer** |
