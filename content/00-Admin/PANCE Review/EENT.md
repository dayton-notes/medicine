### **Eye disorders** 
Conjunctivitis 
- Viral: preauricular adenopathy
- bacterial: topical abx
- allergic: triggered, itching, bilateral
- Newborn ppx: erythromycin (MC: chlamydia)
- G/C conjunctivitis: vision threatening 
Corneal disorders
- 
Cataracts
- lens opacification
Corneal ulcer 
- infection of corneal tissue: MC bacterial
- risks: contact lens use, foreign body, chemical injury
- Tx: topical abx

**Infectious** 
Keratitis
- HSV keratitis: immunocompromised, dendrites w/ fluorescein stain, unilateral ocular pain, oral acyclovir
Pterygium vs Pinguecula
- risks: UV / dust exposure
- pterygium: triangular, crosses edge of iris
- resection only if visual impairment
Inflammatory disorders 
Iritis 
Scleritis 
- "white" part of eye
- Sx: severe, boring pain
- MC: autoimmune, rarely infectious (lyme, syphilis)
- Tx: NSAIDs, steroids/immunomodulators
Uveitis 
- inflammation of iris/choroid/ciliary muscle
- eye pain, photophobia, pupillary constriciton (miosis)
- slit lamp: WBCs, flare
- MC: autoimmune
- Tx: topical steroids

**Lacrimal disorders**
Dacryoadenitis 
- inflammation of superotemporal lacrimal gland
- acute: Viral/bacterial, can ascend from conjunctivitis
- chronic: Sjogren's, sarcoidosis, Crohn's
Dacryocystitis 
- infection of lacrimal sac (medial canthus)
- tearing, erythema, warmth, purulence of inferior medial canthus
- Tx: warm compresses + oral abx
Dacryostenosis 
- obstructed nasolacrimal duct
- epiphoria (tear overflow)
- MC: congenital
- Tx: massage, stenting
Keratoconjunctivitis sicca (KCS)
- chronic dry eyes
- inadequate tear production, Sjögrens
- schirmer test: measures tear production (decreased in KCS)
- Tx: lubricating eye drops

**Lid** **disorders**  
Blepharitis 
- eyelid margin inflammation: sebhorreic, infectious, rosacea
- Anterior: eyelid margin
- Posterior: interior meibomian glands
- Tx: warm compresses, eyelid scrubs
Chalazion  
- typically non-tender
- blockage of internal meibomian glad
- Tx: warm compresses
Ectropion 
- eyelashes out, droopy, excessive tearing
Entropion 
- eyelashes in, foreign body sensation, corneal damage
Hordeolum

Presbyopia

|Feature|**Amblyopia**|**Strabismus**|
|---|---|---|
|Definition|↓ visual acuity from abnormal visual development|Misalignment of eyes (esotropia, exotropia, etc.)|
|Primary problem|**Brain suppression** of one eye|**Extraocular muscle / alignment issue**|
|Eye alignment|Usually normal appearance|**Abnormal** (one eye deviates)|
|Vision|**Decreased** (often unilateral)|May be normal initially|
|Red reflex|Normal|Normal|
|Relationship|Can be **caused by strabismus**|Risk factor for amblyopia|
|Treatment|**Patching** good eye, atropine drops|**Glasses, patching, surgery**|

**Hearing impairment**
Weber Test
- conductive hearing loss lateralizes to PROBLEM ear
- sensorineural hearing loss lateralizes to the GOOD ear
Rinne Test
- bone > air : conductive loss

Conductive (outer/middle ear)
(screening: tuning fork, diagnose: audiogram)

Sensorineural (inner ear)
(screening: tuning fork, diagnose: audiogram)

- trauma
- vestibular neuroma
- idiopathic (high dose oral steroid)
