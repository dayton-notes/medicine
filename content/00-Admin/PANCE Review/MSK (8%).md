# Rheumatologic Disorders

## Connective Tissue Diseases

- **Ehlers-Danlos Syndrome (EDS)**
    
    - COLA / TNXB mutations
        
    - PT referral
        
    - hEDS = clinical diagnosis
        
    - Consider TTE
        
- **Marfan Syndrome**
    
    - FBN1 mutation
        
    - TTE, genetics, ophthalmology eval
        
    - Activity restrictions
        
- **Osteogenesis Imperfecta**
    
    - Blue sclera
        

---

## Fibromyalgia

- > 3 months diffuse pain + somatic symptoms
    
- Associated with anxiety, depression, RLS, OSA
    
- **Treatment:** (multimodal—exercise, CBT, meds)
    

---

## Crystal Arthropathies (Gout / Pseudogout)

- Usually **monoarticular** → arthrocentesis required
    

|Feature|Gout|Pseudogout|
|---|---|---|
|Crystals|Needle-shaped|Rhomboid|
|Birefringence|Negative (blue/purple)|Positive (yellow)|
|Lab|↑ Uric acid|Normal uric acid|
|Classic joint|1st MTP (podagra)|Knee|

- Tophi (gout)
    

**Treatment**

- Acute: NSAIDs, colchicine, steroids
    
- Chronic: allopurinol + lifestyle
    

---

## Juvenile Idiopathic Arthritis (JIA)

- Age <16, arthritis >6 weeks
    
- Scheduled NSAIDs
    

---

## Osteoporosis

- DEXA screening:
    
    - Women ≥65
        
    - Men ≥70
        

---

## Polyarteritis Nodosa

- Medium-vessel vasculitis
    
- Palpable purpura (non-blanching), ulcers, livedo reticularis
    
- Angiography: **“string of pearls / rosary”**
    

---

## Polymyalgia Rheumatica

- Proximal **pain** (shoulder/hip girdle) + stiffness
    
- ~50% associated with giant cell arteritis
    
- ↑ ESR/CRP
    
- **Treatment:** prednisone
    

---

## Polymyositis

- **Proximal muscle weakness**
    
- ↑ CK, anti-Jo-1, aldolase, LFTs, LDH
    
- Diagnosis: EMG/NCS, biopsy
    
- **Treatment:** steroids, DMARDs/biologics
    

---

## Dermatomyositis

- Myositis + skin findings:
    
    - Gottron papules
        
    - Heliotrope rash
        
- Evaluate for **paraneoplastic process**
    

---

## Reactive Arthritis

- Post-infectious (1 week–1 month later)
    
- GI or GU (most commonly **Chlamydia**)
    
- Lower extremity arthritis
    
- Uveitis
    

---

## Rheumatoid Arthritis

- Persistent joint swelling
    
- Ulnar deviation
    
- Morning stiffness >1 hour
    
- Imaging: periarticular osteopenia, marginal erosions
    
- Labs: (RF, anti-CCP, inflammatory markers)
    

---

## Sjögren Syndrome

- Middle-aged women
    
- Sicca symptoms (dry eyes/mouth), parotitis, dental caries
    
- Anti-SSA (Ro), Anti-SSB (La)
    

---

## Systemic Lupus Erythematosus (SLE)

- Women of childbearing age
    

**Manifestations**

- Skin: photosensitivity, malar rash
    
- Joints: arthritis
    
- Kidney: nephritis
    
- Hematologic involvement
    

**Labs**

- ANA ≥1:80
    
- Anti-dsDNA, anti-Smith
    

**Treatment**

- Hydroxychloroquine
    

---

## Systemic Sclerosis (Scleroderma)

- Tight, shiny skin
    
- Anti-Scl-70
    
- Raynaud phenomenon, ILD, pulmonary HTN
    

### CREST (Limited Scleroderma)

- **C**alcinosis
    
- **R**aynaud phenomenon
    
- **E**sophageal dysmotility
    
- **S**clerodactyly
    
- **T**elangiectasia
    
- Anti-centromere antibodies
