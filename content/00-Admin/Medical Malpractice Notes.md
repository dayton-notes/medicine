Outcomes:

MDM Documentation
- Workup findings 
	- Explain all abnormal vitals and lab/imaging findings
- Consultant conversations (with names)
	- especially when they decide to not come see patient
- External historians (families called, external medical practices, etc.)
- Discharge plans (especially when approved/staffed with attending)
