## Step 1 — March 1–30: Applications

1. Obtain your NPI number via [NPPES](https://nppes.cms.hhs.gov/login)
	- Use "student" taxonomy and school address as business/practice address
	- ![[Pasted image 20260311142252.png]]
2. Submit your [NCMB application](https://portal.ncmedboard.org/index.aspx)
    - > **Tip:** Applications are processed in the order they are submitted — apply early!
        
3. Authorize [NCCPA information release](https://portal.nccpa.net/n0/Applications/OpenApps)
	- ![[Pasted image 20260605111523.png]]

---

## Step 2 — April 1–7: Fingerprints

Location: [Forsyth Sheriff Fingerprinting](https://www.forsyth.cc/sheriff/fingerprinting.aspx) (M-F 8:30am - 3:30pm)
1. Obtain [fingerprints](https://portal.ncmedboard.org/Documents/NCMB%20Fingerprint%20Guidance%20Document.pdf) — can take **8–10 weeks** to process
2. Bring both required forms
	- [Applicant Information Form (PDF)](https://portal.ncmedboard.org/Documents/Applicant%20Information%20Form.pdf)
	- [Authority for Release & Electronic Fingerprint Form (PDF)](https://portal.ncmedboard.org/Documents/Authority%20for%20Release%20and%20Electronic%20Fingerprint%20Form.pdf)

> **Goal by Graduation:** Have everything submitted and received/processed by NCMB — _except_ your Graduation and NCCPA verifications.

---

## Step 3 — May/June: Graduation

1. Study for the PANCE
2. Graduate! 
    - Your PA program must send graduation verification to NCMB

---

## Step 4 — PANCE

1. Take the PANCE
2. Pass the PANCE ✓
3. Ensure NCCPA sends your PANCE certification to NCMB

---

> _Waiting period — things are processing..._
> You will receive a series of updates regarding your application
> - NCCPA credentials received
> - Application has moved to quality assurance review

---

## Step 5 — NC Licensed! 

1. Congratulations!
2. Update your NPI taxonomy: _Student_ → _Physician Assistant_
3. Now apply for your DEA license

---

## Step 6 — DEA License

>  **Do NOT apply for DEA until you have your NC state license in hand.**
 
 [DEA CSA Registration Online](https://apps.deadiversion.usdoj.gov/webforms2/spring/main?execution=e1s1)
- NC does not require any additional DEA specific forms beyond your state license
- Complete the required 8-hour Opioid CME (an option: free via [NEJM](https://pain-management-cme.nejm.org/))

---

## Step 7 — Starting Your Job

1. Submit [NC Intent to Practice](https://www.ncmedboard.org/resources-information/professional-resources/online-services/individual-services/pa-forms) form to NCMB
	- Sometime your job will do this for you - verify
2. Update your NPI with your new practice/business address
