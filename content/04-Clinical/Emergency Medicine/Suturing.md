
#### Anesthetics

Max Dose: [Local Anesthetic Dosing Calculator](https://www.mdcalc.com/calc/10205/local-anesthetic-dosing-calculator)

General Adult: 40 mL 1% lidocaine

Choice: lido with/without epi

If the patient does have some other mild allergic reaction such as swelling or pruritic rash, local administration of an **anesthetic within the other group is considered safe**.
