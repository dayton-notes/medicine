## Mechanisms of PPV (CPAP / BIPAP)

1. Airway stenting
	- improves alveolar recruitment
2. LV Afterload ⬇️
	- Lowers LV transmural pressures
	- Raises RV afterload
3. Preload ⬇️
	- decreases venous return
4. Decreases Work of Breathing
	- offloads respiratory muscles
