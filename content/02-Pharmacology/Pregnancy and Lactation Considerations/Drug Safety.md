Pharmacokinetics
•Changes begin in first trimester and peak in second
•Absorption
	–N/V and delayed gastric emptying
	–Increased in gastric pH
•Distribution
	–Increase in maternal plasma volume
	–↑ fat à ↑ Vd of fat soluble medications
	–↓ Albumin à ↑ Vd of highly bound drugs

•

•Metabolism

–Estrogen and progesterone alter liver enzyme activity (increase or decrease elimination)

•Elimination

–↑ GFR by up to 50%

–Unbound drugs more rapidly cleared by kidney and liver

–Estrogen causes cholestasis à slowed clearance of drugs
