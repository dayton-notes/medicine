Methicillin-Resistant S. aureus

Resistant to all* beta-lactam ABX
	Except ceftaroline

Common cause of nosocomial infections (& certain outpatient infections)
	Post-flu [[CAP]]

Antibiotics that cover MRSA:
1. Vancomycin
2. Linezolid
3. Daptomycin
4. Ceftaroline
5. [[Clindamycin]]
6. [[Tetracyclines]]
7. TMP-SMX
8. Dalbavancin
	-great for outpatient MRSA [[Bacteremia]]
	- give 1 dose on day one and another on day 8
	- provides 6-8 weeks of ABX
