Methicillin-Resistant S. aureus

Resistant to all* beta-lactam ABX
	Except ceftaroline

Common cause of nosocomial infections (& certain outpatient infections)
	Post-flu [[CAP]]

Antibiotics that cover MRSA:
1. Vancomycin
2. Linezolid
3. Daptomycin
4. Ceftaroline
5. Clindamycin
6. Tetracyclines
7. TMP-SMX
