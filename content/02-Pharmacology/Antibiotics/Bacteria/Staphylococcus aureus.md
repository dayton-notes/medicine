Intermittently colonized throughout our lives
S. aureus
Diseases:
	From direct inoculation (cuts, punctures, procedures)
		skin - carbuncle, folliculitis, impetigo, cellulitis
		deep infection - surgical wound, osteomyelitis, IV catheter infections
	Blood stream-related
		bacteremia, sepsis
	Metastatic infections - endocarditis, septic arthritis, lung abscess, osteomyelitis
	Toxin-mediated
		toxic shock syndrome, food poisoning
