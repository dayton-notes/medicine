Found in soil, water, plants, and animals (likes a moist environment)
Colonizes sick &/or hospitalized patients at various sites

Usually nosocomial infections

Common infections:  [[CAP|pneumonia]], [[01-Body Systems/Urology/UTI]]

Causes many other infections less commonly including endocarditis, osteo, infections of the eye, skin/soft tissue, & CNS
