

### Aminopenicillins
[[Ampicillin]] – IV/PO

[[Amoxicillin]] – PO (better absorbed)

Coverage:  like natural PCN (Strep, Enterococcus, Listeria) plus:
	E. coli (60%)
	Proteus mirabilis
	H. influenzae (70%)

Clinical Uses
	[[Otitis media]] (Drug of Choice)
	Listeria monocytogenes
	Enterococcus (if susceptible)
	Alternative for Strep. species
	Community-acquired upper respiratory infections (sinusitis)

### Penicillinase Resistant Penicillins

[[Nafcillin]] (IV)
[[Oxacillin]] (IV)
[[Dicloxacillin]] (PO)

Cover [[Staphylococcus aureus]] (MSSA) and most [[Streptococcus]]

Usually drug of choice (DOC) for serious methicillin-susceptible staph infections (along with cefazolin)

Also used for simple cellulitis
