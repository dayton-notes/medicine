
### Prescribing Limits

#### Length of Time:

Acute pain: 5 days 
Post op: 7 days
#### Amount (MME / day)

General: <90 mme / day
- 10mg oxycodone q4 = 90mme
- Non-cancer patients: give w/apap (Norco, etc)
