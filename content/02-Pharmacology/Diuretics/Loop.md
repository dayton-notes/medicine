General Overview: [[Dominating Diuresis]]

| Drug               | Route   |
| ------------------ | ------- |
| Furosemide (Lasix) | PO, IV  |
| Bumetanide (Bumex) | PO, IV  |
| Torsemide          | PO only |
| Ethacrynic Acid    | PO, IV  |
**Side Effects**
“OHH DAANG!”
- Ototoxicity (IV push, high rate)
- Hypokalemia-Hypomagnesemia
- Dehydration
- Allergy (rash)
- Alkalosis (metabolic)
- Nephritis
- Gout (hyperuricemia)

**Primary uses:**
- [[Heart Failure]] (volume overload)
- Hypertension
- Acute/Chronic Hyperkalemia
