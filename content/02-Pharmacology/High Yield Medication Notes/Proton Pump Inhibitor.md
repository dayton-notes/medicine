High yield indications:
- Long-term NSAID use
- High strength NSAID use (toradol / ketorolac)
- Stress ulcer prophylaxis
- GERD

Long Term Use AE's:
- C-diff
- Aspiration [[CAP|Pneumonia]]
- Absorption issues (iron, calcium) and sequelae: osteoporosis, nutritional deficiencies
