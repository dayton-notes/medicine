### Serotonin Withdrawal
 
 Serotonin withdrawal is unpleasant but **not physically dangerous.**
- It is more likely with serotonergics of short half-life:
	- paroxetine (Paxil)
	- venlafaxine (Effexor)
	- desvenlafaxine (Pristiq)
	- fluvoxamine (Luvox)
- Symptoms:
	- Flu-like symptoms
	- Irritiability
	- Headache
	- Lightheadedness
	- Paresthesias (pins-and-needles, tingling)

Some patients may require a long-tail taper over several months to avoid serotonin withdrawal symptoms.
