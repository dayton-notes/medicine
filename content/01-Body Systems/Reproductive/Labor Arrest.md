**Active labor arrest**

- First stage: between onset of labor and full cervical dilation
    - Latent: onset of labor to 6 cm cervical dilation
        - Characterized by slower cervical change, can last up to 20 hours
        
    - Active: 6 cm to full cervical dilation
        
        - Cervix should dilate 1.2–1.5 cm per hour
        - No change for 4 hours with adequate contractions = arrest of labor --> C-section
        - With oxytocin for inadequate contractions --> no change for 6 hours = arrest of labor--> C-section
        
    - Arrest of labor should not be diagnosed if membranes are not ruptured

Quantitative measure of adequate contractions?
- >200 Montevideo units as measured by an intrauterine pressure catheter.
