### quick reference


- Risks: **prior abdominal** or **pelvic surgery**, neoplasm, hernia, abscess
- Sx: **bilious vomiting**, abdominal pain
- PE: possibly high-pitched bowel sounds
- Dx: X-ray with **dilated bowel**, **air-fluid levels**, a **stack of coins** or **string of pearls sign**
    - Diagnosis is made by imaging, most often with CT
- Tx: NPO, IVF, possible NG tube, surgery
