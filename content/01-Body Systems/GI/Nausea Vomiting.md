### PONV
![[Pasted image 20250530163030.png]]

Guidelines:
[Fourth Consensus Guidelines for the Management of Postoperative Nausea and Vomiting](https://www.ashp.org/-/media/assets/policy-guidelines/docs/endorsed-documents/endorsed-documents-fourth-consensus-guidelines-postop-nausea-vomiting.pdf)

1. Assess Risk
	1. Apfel Score
2. Reduce Risk
	1. Multimodal Analgesia
		1. Gastrectomy: IV APAP
		2. NSAIDs/COX-2/Ketamine (intraoperative) have morphine sparing effect
		3. Perioperative Demedetomidine
3. Administer PONV Prophylaxis Using 2 Interventions in Adults at Risk for PONV
	![[Pasted image 20250530162751.png]]
Level A1 combos:
	- Ondansetron plus dexamethasone
Level A2 Evidence combos:
	- Palonosetron plus dexamethasone
	- Ramosetron plus dexamethasone
	- Ondansetron plus aprepitant
	- Ondansetron plus betahistine
	- Aprepitant plus dexamethasone
	- Haloperidol plus midazolam
	- Dexamethasone plus haloperidol

Rescue therapy:

Sniffing isopropyl alcohol wipe!!!
