
### Questions for Assessing Suicidality

- Other people with similar problems sometimes lose hope; have you?  
- This must be a hard time for you; what do you think about when you're feeling down?  
- Do you ever consider running away from your problems?  
- With this much stress, have you thought about hurting yourself?  
- How would you do it?  
- What would happen to your family or significant others if you did that?  
- What has kept you from acting on these thoughts?

### Management of Suicidal ideation

**Non-Imminent Risk  
- First establish safety  
- Offer voluntary commitment  
- Contact family to remove any possible means  
- Contracts are not useful and should be avoided  
**Establish a clinical approach and a Safety Plan  
- Psychotherapy and Pharmacotherapy  
- Increase provider contact  
- Safety plan...

**Imminent risk  
Inpatient:  
- Psych  
- Search room  
- Sitter  
- Capacity assessment?  
**Outpatient:  
- Know how to access emergency psychiatric services and pursue hospitalization.  
- Who can assess capacity?
