### pathophysiology

### epidemiology / etiology  
Viral etiology– parainfluenza (most commonly) but others
Most common in kids 3 months to 3 years, fall and winter
---
### clinical features

•Signs and Symptoms
- Barky cough, stridor, respiratory distress, subclavicular retractions

### diagnosis

Clinical!

---
### other

•Treatment

•Hydration and cool mist humidifier

•Freezer air can relieve symptoms

•Steroids if stridor with exertion

•Dexamethasone 0.6 mg/kg (2-3 day half life)

•Orapred 2mg/kg/day for 3-5 days

•Racemic epi if stridor at rest

Intubation if fails racemic epi
