#### definition


Sepsis III definition of septic shock: Infection causing vasopressor requirement to maintain a MAP > 65 mm (despite fluid resuscitation) _plus_ a serum lactate >2 mM

### treatment

Early (<1 hr) broad spectrum antibiotics
- every hour delay increases mortality
Fluid Resusc (30ml/kg IBW)
- SALT-ED + SMART Trial - LR wins
- Deviate for comorbidities (e.g. less fluid for CHF, etc.)
- Defend the MAP! (>65)
- cap refill, track lactate, urine output, passive leg raising trial (ETCo2 increase by 5% indicates fluid responsiveness)
- Early vasopressors (levophed)
