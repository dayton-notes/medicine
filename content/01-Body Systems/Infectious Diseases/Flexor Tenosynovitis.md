### quick reference

- Kanavel's cardinal signs consist of 4 classic signs of flexor tenosynovitis:
    - Tenderness over the flexor sheath
    - Digit resting in the flexed position
    - Tenderness with passive extension
    - Uniform swelling of the entire digit

### treatment

Nonoperative management consisting only of antibiotic therapy with serial examination:
    - This approach may be considered in patients:
        - Presenting within 48 hours of inoculation
        - Who are otherwise healthy and immunocompetent
        - Without suspicion of abscess or necrosis
    - Improvements in pain and Kanavel signs should be noted within 12-48 hours after the initiation of therapy.
