#### Cardiac Output (CO)

Normal: 4-8 L/min
- <4 L/min: cardiac shock
- >8 L/min: exercise, increased metabolic needs, etc

**CO = SV x HR**

Stroke Volume (SV): product of contractility, afterload, and preload
HR: product of sympathetic and vagal tone


#### Cardiac Index (CI)

CI = CO normalized to Body Surface Area
