### quick reference

Neuroendocrine Tumor Subtype

- Most commonly caused by carcinoid tumors (neuroendocrine tumors that secrete **serotonin**, histamine, catecholamine, prostaglandins, peptides, and other vasoactive substances)
- Sx: **skin flushing**, **diarrhea**, wheezing
- Dx: 24-hour urinary excretion of **5-hydroxyindoleacetic acid**
- Tx: Somatostatin analogs such as octreotide, pasireotide, and lanreotide.
