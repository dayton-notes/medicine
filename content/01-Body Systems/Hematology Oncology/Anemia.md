### etiology  
**Microcytic**
- IDA
- Thalassemias
- Lead/copper
**Macrocytic**
- Hypothyroidism
- Alcohol
- Reticulocytosis (increased avg. size)
- Meds ()
**Megaloblastic (w/ hypersegmented PMNs)**
- B12/Folate deficiency
- Meds that impair DNA synthesis
	- methotrexate
	- TMP-SMX
	- Sulfasalazine

---
### clinical features
**Signs**
- Pallor
- Conjunctival, Mucosa, then skin
- Nail changes
	- Koilonychia (FE deficiency)
- Tachycardia
- Bounding pulse
- Jaundice (hemolytic anemia)

**Symptoms**
- Fatigue
- Weakness
- Dyspnea
- Dizziness
- Pica – Ice, dirt, clay, starch etc.
- Restless Leg Syndrome
- Pulsatile Tinnitus
