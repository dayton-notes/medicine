Definitions:
[[CKD]] is persistently elevated serum Cr + reduced [[GFR]]
	MC cause is [[T2DM]] and/or [[HTN]]

2 things to measure
	Filtration (i.e. GFR)
		 [[Serum Creatinine]] (not elevated until ~%50 reduction in renal function)
		[[Cystatin C]]
		[[BUN]]
			End product of protein metabolism
			85% eliminated by kidneys, 15% by GI tract
			Increases if renal elimination impaired (AKI, CKD), GI bleeding, high protein diet, severe liver disease
			Increases earlier in chronic renal dysfunction than SCr
		
			BUN:Cr Ratio:
			Pre-renal: 20:1
			Intrinsic renal: 10:1
			Upper GIB: >30:1
		
	Failed Protein Reabsorption
		Proteinuria
		Albuminuria
		Cystatin C
